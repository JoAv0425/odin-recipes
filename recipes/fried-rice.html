Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a colorful and flavorful dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, seasonings, and protein. Known for its light smoky aroma and vibrant appearance, it is commonly served at parties and special occasions. Fried rice offers a balanced combination of carbohydrates, vegetables, and protein in a single dish.

Ingidients

  • Long-grain parboiled rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, green peas, sweet corn, green beans)
  • Onions
  • Green bell peppers
  • Vegetable oil or margarine
  • Chicken or beef stock
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cubes or powder
  • Cooked chicken, beef, shrimp, or liver (optional)

Steps

  1. Parboil and cook the rice with stock until partially done.
  2. Stir-fry onions and vegetables in oil.
  3. Add seasoning and spices to the vegetables.
  4. Add the rice and mix thoroughly.
  5. Stir-fry until the rice is evenly coated and fully cooked.
  6. Add protein if desired and mix well.Add protein if desired and mix well.
  7. Serve hot.
